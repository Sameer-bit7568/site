Question & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a robotic course for kids?

A robotics course for kids is a fun, interactive program where children learn to design, build, and program robots. These courses teach problem-solving, coding, and engineering skills, while encouraging creativity and teamwork.

What age group is suitable for a robotic course?

Robotic courses are typically suitable for children aged 6+. The programs are designed to match different skill levels, ensuring that younger kids learn foundational concepts while older students tackle more advanced projects.

Do kids need prior knowledge of robotics or coding?

No prior knowledge of robotics or coding is needed. Our courses are designed for beginners, and we guide students step-by-step from basic to more advanced concepts.

What will kids learn in a robotic course?

In a robotics course, kids will learn to design, build, and program robots. They will gain skills in problem-solving, critical thinking, and teamwork, as well as foundational knowledge of coding and engineering principles.
